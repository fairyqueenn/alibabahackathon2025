# 🥗 Sehatin.ai

**Your AI-powered food assistant for health-conscious living.**

> 🏆 Built in 1 day at **Alibaba Cloud Indonesia Gen AI Hackathon 2025**

---

## 🚀 What It Does

Millions with diabetes, hypertension, and other conditions struggle to choose safe food—especially online.  
**Sehatin.ai** uses AI to make real-time food recommendations and health assessments with local language support.

---

## 🔍 Features

| Feature                   | Description                                                                 |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 🧠 **AI Food Recommendation** | Personalized food suggestions based on health conditions & lifestyle.         |
| 📸 **Upload-Based Analysis**  | Upload food photos to receive nutritional info and a smart health score.      |
